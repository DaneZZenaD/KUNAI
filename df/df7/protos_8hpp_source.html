17 * |
18 * ------------------------------------
19 * |
20 * v
21 * type_list {

24 * } |
25 * |
26 * -------------------------------------
27 * |
28 * v
29 * type_item {
30 * ushort type_idx # type_idx of each member
31 * }
32 *
33 * ProtoID[] protos
34 */
